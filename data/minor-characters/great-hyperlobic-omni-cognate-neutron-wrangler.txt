The Great Hyperlobic Omni-Cognate Neutron Wrangler, the Magic and Indefatigable was a supercomputer from Ciceronicus 12 which Lunkwill said was programmed to be a "fiendish disputer."Dialogue from Fit the Fourth from The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy: The Original Radio Scripts, Douglas Adams, 1985

This computer, according to Deep Thought, could talk all four legs off an Arcturian MegaDonkey, but only Deep Thought could persuade it to go for a walk afterwards.  Radio series
