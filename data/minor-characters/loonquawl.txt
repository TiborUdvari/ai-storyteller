Loonquawl was a descendant of Lunkwill. He, along with Phouchg, presided over the Day of the Answer, seven and a half million years after their ancestors had programmed and turned on Deep Thought.

As one whose entire existence and purpose hinged on this momentous event, he understandably felt rather deflated by the outcome, when Deep Thought gave the answer 42. Radio
